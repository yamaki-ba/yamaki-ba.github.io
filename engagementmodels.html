Engagement Models

We realize that different clients have different needs and constraints. That is why we institute service models that are customized to our client's priorities and strategies.
Our clients work with us on one of the following modes:

CAPABILITY AUGMENTATION: Simplest arrangement.
Our qualified analytics staff would augment your internal capacity on monthly rates.

ANALYTICS PROJECTS: Widely used arrangement.
Requires clearly defined analytics projects - business use case needs to be defined. Delivered by practitioners to practitioners.

MANAGED ANALYTICS: Best value creator for clients.
Natural follow on from project arrangement. We maintain models, track performance and enhancements over time. Provided on a fixed price basis. Resources dedicated to client to work as extension of client organization.

How can you work with us?

1
AD-HOC
  • Ad hoc teams making remote deliveries.
  • Simple standardized analytics requests.
  • Demand generated from multiple sources within the client organization.
  • Irregular volume of work.
  • Coordination with team working on projects not required.
  • Quote provided per request.
2
DEDICATED
  • Dedicated team working for client.
  • Work comprises of combination of simple and complex standardized analytics requests.
  • Steady volume of work.
  • Resources can be deployed on special initiatives during down time.
  • Fixed base +/- variations per request.
3
RETAINER
  • Unclear definition of work and timelines.
  • Work is large in volume and requires project management.
  • Require BiznessAnalytics team to work as outsourced department.
  • Need to scale up the capacity as requirements grow.
  • Fixed price contract renewed annually
